Overview

The world of Lost Omens is under threat from the Light Eaters, creatures that consume all forms of light and bring about darkness. Knowledge and power are vital, and few can command the formidable forces within this chaotic backdrop. Players are called to reclaim the stolen light and venture into forgotten echoes of a time long past.
