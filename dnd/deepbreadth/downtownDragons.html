Languages

Language groups define the accepted name of the spoken language, while dialects are more specific, if not exotic, variations of the overall language group. If available, an entire language group shares the same language script. A character who understands a specific dialect may understand the more prominent language group or language script, but speaking the more extensive language is more challenging.

Erathan (Common)

This is the language most commonly spoken across Hiraeth and is considered the language of the world.  Despite this, several variations exist throughout a region, and dialects create enough differences across cities. This language is the preferred language of Humans and Halflings.
